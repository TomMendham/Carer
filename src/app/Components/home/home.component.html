Welcome to Caring Together


About

Caring Together is an application that supports caregiving by providing a suite of functionality that can be used to manage the day to day role of caring for someone.

timeline

Speed up your working day with easy management, planning, and coordination

healing

Make medication easy with built in monitoring, medication tracking, and health stats

class

Create journal logs to track all activities performed

file_copy

Upload documents, images, and videos to share them easily with other caregivers.
